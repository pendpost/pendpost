# Disclaimer and responsible use

pendpost is a tool for the agent-plus-human social workflow. Please use it responsibly.

## The human approval gate is the posture

pendpost never posts fully autonomously. The human approval gate is the Terms-of-Service posture of this project, not an optional feature: agents draft, lint, schedule, and queue, but a human approves before anything goes live. A post with no approval will not publish, and the actor who created a post cannot approve it. Do not modify pendpost to bypass this gate, and do not present it as an autonomous posting bot. It is not one.

## You own your accounts

You own your platform accounts, and you are responsible for them. Each platform (Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, YouTube, and any others) has its own terms of service and its own automation and acceptable-use policies. It is your responsibility to read and follow each platform's terms, including any rules about automated or assisted posting. pendpost does not exempt you from any of them.

## Anti-ban features reduce risk, they do not eliminate it

pendpost includes anti-ban circuit breakers, a cadence cap, a lane pause kill switch, and the 368 action-block breaker that halts the Meta lane. These reduce the risk of action blocks and account flags. They do not eliminate that risk. Platforms change their rules and their enforcement without notice, and no tool can guarantee your account will not be limited, suspended, or banned. Use conservative cadences and your own judgment.
